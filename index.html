    AI tone review

    Category✔ Good Attribute✖ Bad Attribute
    ClarityDetailed explanation of how the issue was detected, vulnerability details, and remediation stepsTechnical, ambiguous, or unclear warning messages
    TransparencyExplains why the warning occurred and what the threat isNo explanation of why the notification appeared
    ContextProvides context showing why the notification applies to the userLacks context, making the message feel irrelevant
    Decision SupportHelps inform decision-making rather than forcing users to quickly click a buttonPushes users to dismiss without understanding
    GuidanceProvides actionable guidance and remediation stepsNo guidance on what users should do
    Risk CommunicationClearly states potential risks and consequencesDoes not communicate risks clearly
    ConsequencesExplains implications of ignoring the warning and may provide examples of consequencesConsequences are unclear or not mentioned
    User UnderstandingExplains background information so users understand the issueAssumes technical knowledge users may not have
    Encouraging ComplianceClearly communicates the problem and highlights the safer or preferred actionDoes not clearly communicate what action is preferred
    ToneUses constructive or positive framing rather than threats or legal intimidationCreates annoyance or frustration that discourages engagement
    Trust & LegitimacyAppears trustworthy and legitimate to usersLooks suspicious, spam-like, or untrustworthy
    Design & InteractionHas a distinct design or interaction style that differentiates it from normal notificationsLooks identical to regular notifications
    FrequencyReduces unnecessary warnings and only alerts when meaningfulToo many notifications with no meaningful follow-through
    User WorkflowMinimizes disruption and respects the user's workflowInterrupts normal activities, leading users to ignore or disable notifications
    User ImpactIncreases users' perception of security and their intention to continue using the appCauses annoyance, leading users to ignore or dismiss warnings and develop poor security habits
